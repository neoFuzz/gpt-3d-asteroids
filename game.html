GPT 3D Asteroids

Shoot asteroids to score points. Additional Life granted every 10,000 points.

Keyboard controls:

  • Up: Fire thrusters
  • Down: Velocity dampening
  • Left: rotate counter-clockwise
  • Right: rotate clockwise
  • Space: Fire bullets
  • CRTL: warp to a random location on screen

Gamepad controls:

  • Up: Fire thrusters
  • Down: Velocity dampening
  • Left: Rotate counter-clockwise
  • Right: Rotate clockwise
  • Button 1(A): Fire bullets
  • Button 2(B): warp to a random location on screen

Press 'Start' or 'Enter' to ready and 'Fire' to start.
There are a few secrets to discover, can you find them?
